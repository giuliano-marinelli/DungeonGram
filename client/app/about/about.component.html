Dynamic Lighting. Easyly make great playable maps.

Make some walls and doors to achieve the dynamic visibility for the players (also great for dm use too). Add walls, windows or colliders, for fully or partially blocked vision or to make the characters movement avoid obstacles. You can also add interactive doors for make the players to open them slowly and see if there are enemies or not. Moreover you can lock or hide them to players to generate secret doors and hide some tresures!
